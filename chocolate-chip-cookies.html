Chocolate Chip Cookies

Classic homemade cookies that are soft and chewy.

📖 View Recipe 📚 Story Mode

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Mix flour, baking soda, and salt in a bowl.
  3. Cream butter and sugars until light and fluffy.
  4. Beat in eggs and vanilla.
  5. Gradually add dry ingredients to wet mixture.
  6. Stir in chocolate chips.
  7. Drop rounded tablespoons onto ungreased baking sheets.
  8. Bake for 9-11 minutes until golden brown.
  9. Cool on baking sheet for 2 minutes, then transfer to wire rack.

Step 1: Gather Your Ingredients

Assemble all your ingredients. You'll need flour, baking soda, salt, softened butter, both granulated and brown sugar, eggs, vanilla extract, and chocolate chips. Let's make some delicious homemade cookies!

Step 2: Preheat Your Oven

Turn on your oven and set it to 375°F (190°C). Let it preheat for about 10-15 minutes. A properly preheated oven ensures your cookies bake evenly and get that perfect golden-brown color!

Step 3: Mix Dry Ingredients

In a medium bowl, whisk together 2 1/4 cups flour, 1 teaspoon baking soda, and 1 teaspoon salt. This combines the leavening agents evenly throughout the flour. Set this mixture aside for later.

Step 4: Cream the Butter and Sugars

In a large bowl, combine 1 cup softened butter, 3/4 cup granulated sugar, and 3/4 cup brown sugar. Beat them together for about 2-3 minutes until the mixture is light, fluffy, and pale. This incorporates air for tender cookies!

Step 5: Add Eggs and Vanilla

Beat in 2 large eggs and 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract to the butter mixture. Beat well until everything is combined. The mixture will look slightly curdled at first—that's normal! Keep beating until smooth.

Step 6: Combine Wet and Dry

Gradually add the dry ingredient mixture to the wet ingredients, stirring well after each addition. Mix until just combined—don't overmix! Overmixing can make the cookies tough.

Step 6 of 9
← Previous Next Step →

Step 7: Add Chocolate Chips

Fold in 2 cups of chocolate chips into the dough. Stir gently until they're evenly distributed throughout. Yes, you can taste a little bit if you want—quality control is important!

Step 7 of 9

Step 8: Bake the Cookies

Drop rounded tablespoons of dough onto ungreased baking sheets, spacing them about 2 inches apart. Bake for 9-11 minutes until the edges are golden brown but the centers still look slightly underdone. This is the secret to chewy cookies!

Step 8 of 9
← Previous Next Step →

Step 9: Cool and Enjoy!

Let the cookies cool on the baking sheet for 2 minutes, then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely. These warm, soft, chewy chocolate chip cookies are the perfect homemade treat. Enjoy with a cold glass of milk!
