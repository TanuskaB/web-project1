Chicken Stir-Fry

A quick and healthy Asian-inspired dish.

📖 View Recipe 📚 Story Mode

Ingredients

  • 400g chicken breast, sliced
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (broccoli, bell peppers, carrots)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper

Directions

  1. Heat oil in a wok or large pan over high heat.
  2. Add chicken and stir-fry until cooked through.
  3. Add garlic and vegetables, stir-fry for 3-4 minutes.
  4. Add soy sauce and oyster sauce, mix well.
  5. Cook for another 2 minutes until vegetables are tender-crisp.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Serve hot with rice.

Step 1: Prepare Your Ingredients

Slice 400g of chicken breast into bite-sized pieces. Prepare 2 cups of mixed vegetables—chop broccoli, bell peppers, and carrots into similar sizes so they cook evenly. Mince 2 cloves of garlic. Have soy sauce, oyster sauce, salt, pepper, and vegetable oil ready!

Step 2: Heat Your Wok or Pan

Place a wok or large pan on your stove and heat it over high heat. Add 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil. Let it get hot until you see a slight shimmer—this ensures everything will cook quickly and evenly!

Step 3: Cook the Chicken

Add the sliced chicken to the hot wok. Stir-fry it constantly for about 5-7 minutes until it's cooked completely. The chicken should be white/opaque throughout with no pink. Don't move it around too much—let it get a nice sear!

← Previous Next Step →

Step 4: Add the Aromatics

Push the cooked chicken to the side of the wok. Add the minced garlic and stir-fry for about 30 seconds until fragrant. The aroma will be amazing—garlic is the soul of this dish!

← Previous Next Step →

Step 5: Add the Vegetables

Turn up the heat to high and add your prepared vegetables. Stir-fry them constantly for 3-4 minutes. You want them to be colorful and tender-crisp—still with a slight crunch. Don't overcoil them!

Step 5 of 7
← Previous Next Step →

Step 6: Add the Sauce

Pour in 2 tablespoons of soy sauce and 1 tablespoon of oyster sauce. Mix everything well so the sauce coats all the chicken and vegetables. Let it cook for another 1-2 minutes as the flavors combine beautifully!

← Previous Next Step →

Step 7: Season and Serve

Taste your creation and season with salt and pepper as needed. Serve immediately over steamed rice or noodles. This vibrant, healthy dish is packed with flavor and nutrition. Enjoy your homemade Asian-inspired stir-fry!
