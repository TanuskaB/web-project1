About Recipe Studio

Recipe Studio is your all-in-one destination for discovering, creating, and sharing delicious recipes from around the world. Whether you're a beginner in the kitchen or an experienced home chef, Recipe Studio makes cooking simple, enjoyable, and inspiring.

Our platform offers a diverse collection of carefully curated recipes — from quick weekday meals and healthy options to indulgent desserts and international dishes. Each recipe includes clear ingredients, step-by-step instructions, and helpful visuals to guide you every step of the way.


Developers

Tanuska Biswakarma and Caira Major

Recipe Studio was developed by Tanuska Biswakarma and Caira Major, two dedicated developers who collaborated to create a clean, user-friendly, and well-structured recipe management platform. Together, they focused on building an intuitive interface combined with organized and scalable data handling to ensure a smooth and enjoyable user experience.

Tanuska contributed to the overall application structure, data organization, and implementation of core functionalities, ensuring the system remains efficient and maintainable. Caira played a key role in refining the user interface design, enhancing usability, and supporting feature development to create a visually appealing and responsive platform.

Through teamwork, attention to detail, and a shared commitment to quality, they successfully developed Recipe Studio as a practical and scalable web application that can be expanded with additional features in the future.
