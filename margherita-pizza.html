Margherita Pizza

Classic Italian pizza topped with tomato sauce, mozzarella, and basil.

📖 View Recipe 📚 Story Mode

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 200g fresh mozzarella
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over dough.
  4. Add sliced mozzarella.
  5. Bake for 10-12 minutes until crust is golden.
  6. Top with fresh basil and drizzle olive oil.
  7. Slice and serve.

Step 1: Gather Your Ingredients

Prepare for an authentic Italian pizza experience! You'll need 1 ball of pizza dough, 1/2 cup tomato sauce, 200g fresh mozzarella, fresh basil leaves, olive oil, and salt. These simple, quality ingredients are the secret to a classic Margherita!

Step 2: Preheat Your Oven

Turn on your oven and set it to 475°F (245°C). Let it preheat for about 15-20 minutes until it reaches the proper temperature. A hot oven is essential for creating a crispy crust with a soft, chewy interior!

Step 3: Prepare the Dough

Dust a clean work surface with flour. Take your pizza dough and gently stretch and roll it out into a circle about 12-14 inches wide. The dough should be thin but sturdy enough to hold the toppings. If the dough springs back, let it rest for a minute, then try again.

Step 3 of 7
← Previous Next Step →

Step 4: Add the Sauce

Place your rolled dough on a pizza pan or baking stone. Spread 1/2 cup of tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges for the crust. Don't overload with sauce—this is a simple pizza where each ingredient shines!

Step 4 of 7
← Previous Next Step →

Step 5: Add the Cheese

Slice or tear 200g of fresh mozzarella into bite-sized pieces. Distribute the mozzarella evenly over the tomato sauce, but don't cover every inch—leave some sauce visible. This creates pockets of cheese and sauce that flavor every bite!

Step 5 of 7
← Previous Next Step →

Step 6: Bake the Pizza

Carefully place your pizza in the preheated oven. Bake for 10-12 minutes until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbling at the edges. Your kitchen will smell absolutely amazing! Keep an eye on it to prevent burning.

Step 6 of 7
← Previous Next Step →

Step 7: Finish and Serve

Remove the pizza from the oven (carefully—it's hot!). Immediately top it with fresh basil leaves and drizzle with 1 tablespoon of good olive oil. Let it cool for a minute, then slice and serve. This authentic Margherita pizza is pizza perfection!
