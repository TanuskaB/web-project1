Vegetable Omelette

A quick and healthy breakfast packed with fresh vegetables.

📖 View Recipe 📚 Story Mode

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1/4 cup bell peppers, diced
  • 1/4 cup onions, diced
  • 1/4 cup spinach
  • 2 tbsp milk
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • 1 tbsp butter

Directions

  1. Whisk eggs, milk, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  2. Heat butter in a pan over medium heat.
  3. Sauté vegetables for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Pour egg mixture over vegetables.
  5. Cook until edges set, then fold in half.
  6. Cook another minute until done.
  7. Serve warm.

Step 1: Gather Your Ingredients

Prepare your ingredients for this healthy, delicious omelette. You'll need 3 eggs, 1/4 cup diced bell peppers, 1/4 cup diced onions, 1/4 cup spinach, 2 tablespoons milk, butter, salt, and black pepper. Fresh vegetables make all the difference!

Step 2: Prepare the Vegetables

Dice your bell peppers and onions into bite-sized pieces. Wash the spinach and roughly chop it. Having all your vegetables prepped and ready makes the cooking process quick and easy. Mise en place—everything in its place!

Step 3: Whisk the Eggs

In a bowl, whisk together 3 eggs, 2 tablespoons milk, salt, and black pepper. Whisk until the mixture is light and well combined. The milk helps create a fluffier, more delicate omelette.

Step 3 of 7
← Previous Next Step →

Step 4: Heat Your Pan

Place a non-stick skillet over medium heat. Add 1 tablespoon of butter and let it melt until it foams. You want the pan to be hot enough to cook but not so hot that things burn. Butter creates flavor and helps prevent sticking.

Step 4 of 7
← Previous Next Step →

Step 5: Sauté the Vegetables

Add your diced bell peppers and onions to the buttered pan. Sauté for 2-3 minutes until they're softened and fragrant. The vegetables will become tender and release their flavors into the butter. Cooking them first ensures they're perfectly tender in your omelette.

Step 5 of 7
← Previous Next Step →

Step 6: Cook the Egg Mixture

Pour your whisked egg mixture over the sautéed vegetables in the pan. Let it cook for 1-2 minutes without stirring until the edges begin to set. Add the spinach now. When the edges are set but the middle is still slightly wet, fold the omelette in half.

Step 6 of 7
← Previous Next Step →

Step 7: Finish and Serve

Cook the folded omelette for another minute until the inside is cooked through but still slightly soft—not rubber-like! Slide your beautiful vegetable omelette onto a plate and serve immediately. This nutritious, delicious breakfast is healthy and satisfying!
