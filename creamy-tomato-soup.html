Creamy Tomato Soup

A warm and comforting tomato soup with a creamy texture.

📖 View Recipe 📚 Story Mode

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 800g canned crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Directions

  1. Heat olive oil in a pot over medium heat.
  2. Sauté onion until soft.
  3. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute.
  4. Stir in crushed tomatoes and broth.
  5. Simmer for 15 minutes.
  6. Blend until smooth.
  7. Stir in cream, season, and serve warm.

Step 1: Gather Your Ingredients

Prepare for a warm, comforting soup experience! You'll need 2 tablespoons of olive oil, 1 onion (chopped), 2 cloves of garlic (minced), 800g of canned crushed tomatoes, 2 cups of vegetable broth, 1/2 cup of heavy cream, salt, and black pepper. Let's create soup magic!

Step 2: Heat the Oil

Pour 2 tablespoons of olive oil into a large pot and heat it over medium heat. Let it warm for about a minute. The oil will be ready when it shimmers slightly. You want it hot enough to cook but not smoking.

Step 3: Sauté the Onion

Add your chopped onion to the hot oil. Stir occasionally and cook for about 5 minutes until the onion becomes soft and translucent. As it cooks, it will release its natural sugars and create a sweet, savory base for your soup!

Step 3 of 7
← Previous Next Step →

Step 4: Add Garlic

Add your minced garlic to the softened onion and stir constantly for about 1 minute. The garlic will become fragrant and golden. This combination of onion and garlic is the classic flavor foundation of countless soups!

Step 4 of 7
← Previous Next Step →

Step 5: Add Tomatoes and Broth

Pour 800g of canned crushed tomatoes and 2 cups of vegetable broth into the pot. Stir everything together and bring it to a simmer. The soup should bubble gently, not boil vigorously. Let it simmer for 15 minutes as flavors develop and deepen.

Step 5 of 7
← Previous Next Step →

Step 6: Blend Until Smooth

After 15 minutes of simmering, it's time to blend! Carefully transfer the soup to a blender (working in batches if needed) and blend until completely smooth. Or use an immersion blender directly in the pot. A smooth texture is the hallmark of creamy tomato soup!

Step 6 of 7
← Previous Next Step →

Step 7: Finish with Cream and Serve

Pour the blended soup back into the pot (if you transferred it). Stir in 1/2 cup of heavy cream, which adds richness and transforms the soup into something luxurious. Season with salt and black pepper to taste. Serve warm and enjoy the comfort of homemade tomato soup!
